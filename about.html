About Me

Hello! I'm David McHugh Thompson, but you can call me Mac. I've got a wide variety of interests, but there's a couple commonalities between them that ties them together. I enjoy things that force me to solve problems and improve myself to get better at them, and that give me a sense of fulfillment and purpose. For example, I love exercising, in particular weightlifting, biking and hiking. I enjoy how they make me push myself, and I enjoy the feeling of pride and accomplishment of overcoming a personal limitation and of exercising the abilities I've worked to develop.

In the same vein, I enjoy cooking and playing fighting games (Play Melee and Rev 2!). Both of those hobbies require discipline and practice to cultivate skill, as well as the ability to honestly and accurately reflect on one's shortcomings and weaknesses and make a deliberate effort to overcome them. Those problem solving skills; as well as the confidence in my ability to learn that those skills have granted me, have served me very well in my endavours to learn other difficult skills. My hobbies, studies, and natural disposition mean that I have several skills that make me a valuable addition to a team. I studied philosophy in college, and as a result, I am a good problem solver, a quick learner, and overall am quite intelligent and capable of articulating complex concepts. I communicate well with others, I am good at working with others in a team setting, and I am adept at distilling complex concepts down into their simplest components. I've spent some years in the foodservice industry before learning how to code, so I am adept at working hard and recognizing when something simply must be taken care of.
